Travel

Denver International Airport
8500 Peña Blvd, Denver, CO 80249, USA

This is the closest major airport! It is 45 Minutes to the recommended hotels. Car rentals are available, as is the regional bus system, Uber, Greenride, etc.

Directions
The Church

The address for the church is  1820 15th St, Boulder, CO 80302. The ceremony will be in the Chapel, which has entrances on the northeast corner of the building. The city parking garage north of the church is free on Saturdays, so we recommend parking there. 

The Gatehouse

The address for the reception is 1055 S 112th St, Lafayette, CO 80026. We will be in The Gatehouse, which is on the north half of the Lionsgate Event Center. There is plenty of parking on site. It will take about 25 minutes to travel here from the church.
